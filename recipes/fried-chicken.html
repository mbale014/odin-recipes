Fried chicken

Description

A Delicious fried chicken and simple to make together with your kids. A classic recipe everyone can enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons dry potato flakes
  • 1 tablespoon seasoned salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 30 saltine crackers
  • 1 egg
  • 6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 2 cups vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Place crackers in a large resealable plastic bag; seal bag and crush crackers with a rolling pin until they are coarse crumbs. Add thee flour, potato flakes, seasoned salt, and pepper then mix well.
  2. Beat egg in a bowl. One by one, dredge chicken piecees in egg, then place in bag with crumb mixture. Seal bag and shake to coat
  3. Heat oil in deep fryer or large saucepan to 175 degrees C
  4. Fry chicken, turning frequently, until golden brown and juices run clear, 15 to 20 minutes.
