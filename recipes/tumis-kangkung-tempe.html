Tumis kangkung tempe
(stir-fried water spinach and tempeh)

Description

Tumis kangkung tempeh is a traditional Indonesian side dish that combine stir fried water spinach , tempeh with oyster sauce and seasoning. Usually Indonesian served this vegetarian food together with main dish like fried fish or fried chicken.

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch of water spinach, sliced
  • 2 pieces of tempeh, cut into pieces
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oils
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon of seasoning (mushroom powder/chicken powder/MSG)
  • 2 cloves onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 2 red chile peppers. chopped
  • half cup water

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or sizeable pan over high heat.
  2. Saut√© the onion and garlic. Cook and stir until onion is soft. Pour and stir tempeh, chile peppers; mix well. Cook and stir for about 1 minute.
  3. Add the oyster sauce, seasoning and a little water, then taste. Mix in water spinach and cook until wilted, 3-5 minutes.
  4. Serve while warm.
